### Project Description
Arogya is a safe online community where women can ask health-related questions anonymously and receive responses from verified doctors and supportive users. The platform focuses on privacy, trust, and easy access to health discussions.

### The Problem statement
Many women hesitate to ask personal health questions due to privacy concerns, social stigma, or lack of reliable medical guidance online. This often leads to confusion or delayed help.

### The Solution
Arogya provides a secure platform where users can post questions anonymously and doctors can respond with verified medical guidance. The platform organizes discussions into categories like Periods, Mental Health, Hormonal Changes, and General Wellness.
